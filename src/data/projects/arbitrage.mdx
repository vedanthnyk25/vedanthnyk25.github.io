## Overview
This bot is designed to find and act on price discrepancies between different currency pairs. It leverages advanced graph algorithms and high-speed data ingestion to identify profitable trades before the market corrects.

## Key Features
- **Bellman-Ford Algorithm**: Implemented for detecting negative cycles in currency exchange graphs, identifying arbitrage paths.
